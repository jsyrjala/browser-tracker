Web Tracker

RuuviTracker compatible tracker that sends location of your browser to RuuviTracker server.

  1. Click Start Tracking -> browser will send your location to RuuviTracker every few moments.
  2. If you see something interesting or have some thoughts to share, write a message to the box. The message will be attached to your location.
  3. When you don't feel like to be tracked any more, click Stop Tracking
